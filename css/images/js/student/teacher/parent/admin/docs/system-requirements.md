# Project Overview

DreamTech International Virtual Academy is a technology-driven virtual learning institution designed to provide high-quality Competency-Based Education (CBE) through a comprehensive digital learning ecosystem.

The platform will serve Junior School and Senior School learners through online learning, collaboration tools, artificial intelligence support, and academic management systems.

The objective is to create a world-class virtual academy that provides flexible, accessible, engaging, and future-ready education.

---

# Vision

To become Africa's leading virtual academy, empowering learners through innovative technology, personalized education, and global learning opportunities while fostering academic excellence, creativity, and lifelong learning.

---

# Mission

To provide high-quality, learner-centered virtual education through innovative technology, highly qualified educators, and engaging learning experiences that inspire excellence, nurture talents, and prepare learners to succeed in a rapidly changing global society.
