About DreamTech

DreamTech International Virtual Academy is a technology-driven institution designed to provide high-quality Competency-Based Education (CBE) through a comprehensive digital learning ecosystem. Our academy combines innovative teaching methods, interactive virtual classrooms, academic excellence, and personalized learner support to create an engaging educational experience for students in Junior School and Senior School. We believe that every learner deserves access to quality education regardless of location or circumstance.

Vision

To become Africa's leading virtual academy, empowering learners through innovative technology, personalized education, and global learning opportunities.

Mission

To provide high-quality learner-centered education through technology, qualified educators, and engaging learning experiences that prepare learners for success in a rapidly evolving world.
